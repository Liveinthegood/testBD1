I LOVED YOU MARYLAND

After A&P, I found myself to have the great fortune to join the design team for Shoppers Supermarket (Super Valu) in Bowie, MD. I worked for Ivie & Associates officially. (based out of Flower Mound, TX which is by Dallas.) They assigned me to this satellite location by Washington, D.C. Here also, I designed circulars and created more publications which added value daily. I even designed pages for Shop N Save and Farmfresh as well as spearheaded the El Tiempo Latino Full Page Newspaper Ad project during this time. (for Shoppers, through the Washington Post Newspaper.)

"I throughly enjoyed my time working with Bill, and truly came to know him as an asset to my team. He is creative, timely and dependable. Bill was always inspired by a challenge and often exceeded expectations in his designs. Along with his undeniable talents, Bill has always been an absolute joy to work with. He was a real asset to the team and collaborated well with his teammates as well as the client."
Darin Warren,
Director of Marketing,
Ivie & Associates

I was only there for two years because they packed up this satellite location to join again the Shoppers Corporate Office and “moved shop” to MN. They already had a full staff in MN so I moved back north to join forces with Clark Printing in Fairfield, NJ as my next adventure awaited...

IN JUNE OF 2017, I won a "Stewie" Award or Stewardship Award, As stated by Ivie & Associates:
“As a winner, you demonstrated the leadership role you play with your team regarding culture. You displayed a positive attitude and encouraged others to achieve their personal best.”
